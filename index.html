Hi!

I am Pratham Oza, a PhD student in the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department at Virginia Tech. I work with Dr. Thidapat (Tam) Chantem at the RTX Lab. My research focus is in the areas of Intelligent Transportation, specifically in Traditional/ Automated Vehicular Traffic Scheduling using real-time systems theories. I completed my Bachelors degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from Manipal Institute of Technology, Manipal and my Masters degree at Virginia Tech in 2019.

Prior to joining Virginia Tech, I worked for about two years with Tata Consultancy Services and a Japanese OEM on vehicular ECU diagnostics and ECU test validation. I have also briefly worked on HILS testing of the Meter ECU in a Japanese OEM vehicle using dSPACE, Vector CANoe and LabView.

Feel free to contact me at prathamo[at]vt[dot]edu for questions or comments.
